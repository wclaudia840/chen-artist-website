Wang Chen explores her feministic insight through unique style of video art and small-scale installations. Her inspiration comes from her family history and her internal memories, as well as delicate emotions she observes and captures in everyday life. In her artworks, Wang Chen aims to show emotional, universal resonance that transcends cultural backgrounds.
